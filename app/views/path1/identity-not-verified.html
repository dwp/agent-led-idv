Customer identity not verified

The IDV check has not been completed

The customer has not been able to verify their identity. You should record the outcome of this identity check in the relevant service line system.

What to do next

As the customer has failed the IDV check, you must:

  • tell the customer that you are ending the call, and
  • ask the customer to call back when they have access to the correct information, or
  • give the customer the option to write in and make sure they have the correct address if they do not wish to call back.

Customers have two separate attempts to pass the IDV check. If they fail on the second attempt, seek advice from your team leader.

If this is their second failed IDV check

You must record this outcome in the relevant service line system.

A bogus call report must be considered and sent as appropriate to the relevant security group inbox. Find out more about link text (opens in new tab)

You should use your judgement to decide whether any alternative arrangementscan be made, for example:

  • an outbound call
  • face to face, for example a visit or a check at the office
  • requests for documents to be sent by post
If you are inactive for more than 2 minutes, this page will time out and return to the start of the IDV process.

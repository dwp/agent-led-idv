Establish identity

You need to match the customer to their DWP record

Before you can verify the claimant's identity (ID), you need to establish their identity by matching them to their DWP claimant record.

To do this, you should ask:

  • What is your last name?
  • What is your first name?
  • What is your National Insurance number?
Additional questions available

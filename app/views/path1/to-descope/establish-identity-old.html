Step 1

Establish identity

Before you can verify the customers identity (ID) you need to match the claimed ID to their DWP customer record.

Action – establish identity questions

The following questions, including historical values where appropriate, will help to match the customer to their DWP record:

You should ask:

  • What is your last name?
  • What is your first name?
  • What is your date of birth?
  • What is your address?
  • What is your National Insurance number (NINO) or identity code?

You must ask enough questions to satisfy that you have matched the customer to the correct DWP record.

Additional questions available

The following questions can also be used, if needed, to establish the customers ID:

  • What is your home telephone number?
  • What is your mobile telephone number?
  • What is your email address?

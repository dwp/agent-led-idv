Step 2

Fraud check

A fraud check must be completed:

  • during customer contact
  • during claim processing
  • before claim completion, or
  • before any payment impact.

Action

You must preview the claimant’s personal details in CIS Searchlight for the following fraud indicators:

Action - Check date of death held

  • where identity verification (IDV) is passed you must investigate whether the date of death has been incorrectly recorded on CIS Searchlight, learn about Part 2 - General and Specialist Procedures 
  • where IDV is not passed you must complete a fraud referral

You must make sure the claimed identity is not:

  • at a higher risk than usual of identity fraud
  • suspected to be a synthetic or made-up identity.

Where an ID @ Risk Flag is held in CIS, take specific action detailed within the Knowledge based verification questions guidance to verify the customers identity.

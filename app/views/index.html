Research goal

In this round, we wanted to understand if:

  • Whether the concept of a guided user interface could/would be used by agents in live work.
  • Test perceived value for consistency, compliance and the overall usefulness of such a tool.
  • Understand work set-ups and systems to inform how this tool could fit into their workflow.
  • Explore current behaviour around fraud indicators and risk.

Guided Light UI v1.0

Our design thinking has started with how a stand alone light touch guided UI can support compliance with the IDV operational instructions. Work is ongoing whether this journey will be iterated or that we will move onto a more feature rich tool.

We will focusing on the following:

  • Linear checklist view of the IDV process broken into sequential stages
  • Explain what is matching or establishing identity
  • Static text guidance for what to do step by step
  • Embedded policy reminders or tooltips that reinforce importance of IDV
  • Guidance links for more complex scenarios (not in scope V1.0)
  • Checkboxes for personal tracking to remind that each stage is done (but not stored)
  • Critical messaging to the users, interruption pages or notifications

Quick win for Colleague View?

Simply insert notification banners into their journey. This is would act as an alert or reminder for the user to perform the manual IDV checks.

Important

You must perform identity verification before continuing with the call

Please take the actions described below:

Step 1 - Establish identity

Before you can verify the customers identity (ID) you need to match the claimed ID to their DWP customer record.

You should ask:

  • What is your last name?
  • What is your first name?
  • What is your date of birth?
  • What is your address?
  • What is your National Insurance number (NINO) or identity code?

You must ask enough questions to satisfy that you have matched the customer to the correct DWP record.

Important

You must verify the callers identity by asking 2 from 3 knowledge based verification questions.

  • Do not use new claim data for first-time claimants
  • Use historical data where possible
  • Choose questions from 2 different systems if available.

KBVs from CIS Searchlight

  • "Name another benefit or DWP payment you receive." (A 'none' response is acceptable)
  • "Name another benefit you have claimed previously, regardless of whether it was paid to you?"
  • "What was your previous surname?"
  • "What were your previous addresses?" (First line of address and town are acceptable, postcode not needed).
  • "On which date did your claim for the other benefit start?" (The correct month and year is acceptable).
  • ""What is the weekly or monthly amount of the other benefit?" (A reasonable tolerance range, depending on the amount, may be applied).
  • "When did you receive your last payment?"
  • "What is your partner’s National Insurance number?"
  • "What is your partner’s full name?"
  • "What is your partner’s date of birth?"
  • "How many dependents are in your claim?"
  • "What is the name of one of your dependent children?"
  • "What is the date of birth of one of your dependent children?"

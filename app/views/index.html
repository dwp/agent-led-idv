Identity Checker prototypes

The aim of Identity Checker is give DWP agents across service lines a digital tool to better deliver business compliant identity Verification when dealing with claimants over different channels. While in Alpha we are working towards defining which solution we will take forward as our minimum viable product (MVP).

Design progress

KBVUR1 Completed
December 2025

UR 1 - KBV prototype options testing

Focused prototype testing with the Later Life Team to look at two alternatives on how we surface KBV questions.

Option 1 - Agent is shown question and answer

Agent is tasked with asking questions of the customer. Then comparing their responses and accuracy against the answers shown.

Prototype will give both sucess and fail outcomes based on selections.

View prototype option A

Option 2 - Agent is shown question only (blind check)

Agent is tasked with asking questions of the customer then selecting or inputting the answers given. The system will then decide on accuracy.

Please refer to the instructions below before using prototype option B.

View prototype option B
Important instructions for option B flow

To view success after two correct questions only

  • Q1 - Select correct benefit, Job seekers allowance
  • Q2 - Enter correct date 25-10-2025
  • Customer verified

To view success after two from three correct questions

  • Q1 - Select correct benefit, Job seekers allowance
  • Q2 - Enter incorrect date such as 12-11-2023
  • Q3 - Enter correct mobile number 07123456789 (allows for spaces)
  • Customer verified

To view failure after two from three incorrect questions

  • Q1 - Select correct benefit, Job seekers allowance
  • Q2 - Enter incorrect date such as 12-11-2023
  • Q3 - Enter incorrect mobile number such as 07581234560 (allows for spaces)
  • Customer not verified

Iteration 1Design In progress
September 2025

Guided Light UI v1.0

Our design thinking has started with how a stand alone light touch guided UI can support compliance with the IDV operational instructions. Work is ongoing whether this journey will be iterated or that we will move onto a more feature rich tool.

We will focusing on the following:

  • Linear checklist view of the IDV process broken into sequential stages
  • Explain what is matching or establishing identity
  • Static text guidance for what to do step by step
  • Embedded policy reminders or tooltips that reinforce importance of IDV
  • Guidance links for more complex scenarios (not in scope V1.0)
  • Checkboxes for personal tracking to remind that each stage is done (but not stored)
  • Critical messaging to the users, interruption pages or notifications

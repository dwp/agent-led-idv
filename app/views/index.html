Overview

The aim of Identity Checker is to give DWP agents across service lines a digital tool to better deliver business compliant identity Verification when dealing with claimants over different channels. While in Alpha we are working towards defining which solution we will take forward as our minimum viable product (MVP).

Although we try to cover all paths throughout the prototype, as its constantly being worked on, some links may be broken or parts of the prototype not work as expected.

Important instructions for option KBV questions

To view success after two correct questions only

  • Q1 - Select correct benefit, Job seekers allowance
  • Q2 - Enter correct date 25-10-2025
  • Customer verified

To view success after two from three correct questions

  • Q1 - Select correct benefit, Job seekers allowance
  • Q2 - Enter incorrect date such as 12-11-2023
  • Q3 - Enter correct mobile number 07123456789 (allows for spaces)
  • Customer verified

To view failure after two from three incorrect questions

  • Q1 - Select correct benefit, Job seekers allowance
  • Q2 - Enter incorrect date such as 12-11-2023
  • Q3 - Enter incorrect mobile number such as 07581234560 (allows for spaces)
  • Customer not verified

September 2025

Light touch guided UI

It became apparent during in Alpha that building in effect a digital version of the existing guidance wasn't going to give any real value to the agent compared to their current process.

Looking back our design thinking started with how a stand alone light touch guided UI could support compliance with the IDV operational instructions.

Features

  • Linear checklist view of the IDV process broken into sequential stages
  • Explain what is matching or establishing identity
  • Static text guidance for what to do step by step
  • Embedded policy reminders or tooltips that reinforce importance of IDV
  • Guidance links for more complex scenarios (not in scope V1.0)
  • Checkboxes for personal tracking to remind that each stage is done (but not stored)
  • Critical messaging to the users, interruption pages or notifications

December 2025

KBV show answers

We had two options for how KBVs would be implemented in the UI. One was to show both the question and answer to the agent and one which showed the answers only. Although showing the answers did have some benefits in supporting agents to apply their tolerance and expereince in accepting answers.

But after successive UR it became obvious that the more secure 'Full fat' version, with the question only was the preferred option which in addition better aligned with KBVaaS online One Login journey.
